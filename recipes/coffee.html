Coffee

Description

Just your standard cup of hot coffee. This is something I look forward to every morning, and in many cases, also the night prior to my coffee. Its a ritual. Its a friend. Its a tool. Its a crutch. What's not to love?

This recipe is going to be mostly unhelpful, but there is a chance that is some amount helpful to someone who has never made coffee before. Good luck.

Ingredients

  • Coffee grounds, Dunkin if possible
  • Hot water
  • Coffee filter
  • Mug

Steps

  1. Heat water
  2. Pour water over coffee grounds and through filter
  3. Pour your coffee from your device into a mug
  4. Drink the coffee at preferred speed
