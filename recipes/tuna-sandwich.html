Tuna Sandwich

Description

This is a recipe for your run of the mill tuna sandwich. Nothing special, nothign stinky, just a tuna sandwich

I've been eating these my whole life. You can either toast the bread or go raw. Dealer's choice.

Ingredients

  • Sourdough bread
  • A can of tuna fish
  • Mayonnaise
  • Dijon mustard
  • Celery
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Put bread on a flat surface
  2. Mash together the tuna fish, mayonnaise, mustard and celery
  3. Season with salt and pepper
  4. Place tuna salad on the bread
  5. Complete the sandwich by placing the 2nd piece of bread on top of the now shmeared slice
